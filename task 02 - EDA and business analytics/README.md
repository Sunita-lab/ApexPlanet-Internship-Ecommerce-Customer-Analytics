# Task 2: Exploratory Data Analysis & Business Insights

## Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), data cleaning, feature engineering, SQL-based business analysis, and generating actionable business insights from an E-Commerce Sales dataset.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that can support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer demographics, product information, order details, and sales transactions.

### Key Features

- Order_ID
- Order_Date
- Customer_ID
- Customer_Name
- Age
- Gender
- City
- Product
- Category
- Quantity
- Unit_Price
- Total_Sales

---

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Quality Assessment

- Identified missing values
- Checked duplicate records
- Examined data types
- Generated descriptive statistics

### 2. Data Cleaning

- Imputed missing Age values using median
- Imputed missing City values using mode
- Validated data consistency
- Verified Total Sales calculations

### 3. Feature Engineering

Created additional analytical features:

- Month (derived from Order_Date)
- Age_Group (customer segmentation)

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

#### Univariate Analysis

- Age Distribution
- Quantity Distribution
- Total Sales Distribution
- Product Category Distribution

#### Bivariate & Multivariate Analysis

- Revenue by Category
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Gender
- Sales by Age Group
- Sales by City
- Top Products by Revenue
- Top Customers by Revenue

#### Correlation Analysis

- Correlation Heatmap
- Relationship between Quantity, Unit Price, and Total Sales

---

## SQL Business Analysis

The cleaned dataset was loaded into an SQLite database and analyzed using SQL queries.

### Business Questions Answered

1. Which product generated the highest revenue?
2. Which category generated the highest revenue?
3. Who are the top 10 customers by spending?
4. Which city generated the highest sales?
5. What is the monthly sales trend?
6. How much revenue was generated by each gender?
7. What is the average order value (AOV)?

---

## Key Findings

- Electronics generated the highest overall revenue.
- Laptop was the highest revenue-generating product.
- Patna recorded the highest sales among all cities.
- Customers aged 36–45 contributed the most revenue.
- Male customers generated slightly higher revenue than female customers.
- Sales showed seasonal peaks during March, June, November, and December.
- Quantity and Unit Price showed strong positive relationships with Total Sales.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Prioritize Electronics inventory and promotions.
- Focus retention efforts on high-value customers.
- Increase marketing activities during high-performing months.
- Develop targeted campaigns for the 36–45 age segment.
- Expand successful product strategies to lower-performing categories.
